For as long as language has existed, people have shared stories. In our modern age, this has grown to the websites and applications we use in our daily lives.

The term Creepypasta comes from the combination of the phrase Copypasta, a story on the internet that is copy and pasted to various message boards and sites, and creepy, indicating the dark nature of the stories being told.
